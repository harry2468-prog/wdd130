Our Trips

We offer a variety of trips ranging from beginner-friendly calm waters to advanced whitewater challenges. All trips include professional guides, life jackets, and safety briefings.

Join us for a half-day or full-day adventure with breathtaking scenery and thrilling rapids.

Equipment

We provide top-quality rafts, paddles, helmets, and life jackets. Our guides ensure everyone has the proper safety gear before starting the trip.
