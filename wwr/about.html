History

Early Origins: Whitewater rafting traces its earliest roots to ancient civilizations. Egyptians used papyrus rafts for transportation, while other cultures built log rafts to navigate rivers for travel and trade.

Modern Growth: By the mid-20th century, rafting expanded rapidly through commercial trips and improved equipment. Today, it is a global adventure sport emphasizing safety, eco-friendly practices, and recreation.
